The Photographer

Leyla Mustafoski

I'm a photographer based in New York and New Jersey. I photograph the occasions people will want to remember — weddings, milestones, celebrations, and portraits.

About

I've been photographing people and their milestones for several years, working across New York City, New Jersey, and beyond. Over that time I've covered hundreds of events — from intimate ceremonies to large receptions, Sweet 16s, communions, family portraits, and everything in between.

My approach is documentary. I photograph what's actually happening rather than constructing it. That means I move quietly, stay out of the way when I should, and step in when I need to — but the goal is always photographs that feel true to the day rather than performed for the camera.

I'm based in New York and New Jersey and photograph throughout the tri-state area. For destination events and travel, reach out and we'll work out the details.
